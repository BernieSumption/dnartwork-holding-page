Step 1: get gene tested

In order to get a DNArtwork, you need a gene test from one of our supported providers: Ancestry.com, 23andme and National Geographic. If you have a gene test from a different provider then contact me on bernie@berniecode.com - I may be able to add support for your provider.

Step 2: provide your genetic data

IMPORTANT! By using this page you are agreeing to the entirely reasonable Terms & Conditions. You should actually read them. They're very short.

First you need to download the raw data from your provider. For instructions, try these links: Ancestry.com, 23andme, National Genographic and Family Tree DNA. If you're having trouble with this step, contact your gene test provider for help.

Next use the button below to provide the raw gene data for analysis. The files are analysed by this web page, and no genetic data will leave your computer. Read more about how your DNA is analysed here.
